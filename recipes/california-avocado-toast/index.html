Overview

For added flavor, try using a drizzle of balsamic glaze or a sprinkle of everything bagel seasoning. You can also add a poached egg on top for extra protein. This fresh, vibrant dish brings the taste of California straight to your table in under 10 minutes.

Ingredients

2
  • 4 slices bread whole grain or sourdough
  • 2 avocados ripe
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice freshly squeezed
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 8 cherry tomatoes halved
  • 2 tbsp feta cheese crumbled, optional
  • 1 tbsp olive oil extra virgin
  • salt to taste
  • black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1 Toast the bread slices in a toaster or on a griddle until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  2. 2 While bread is toasting, cut avocados in half, remove pit, and scoop flesh into a bowl.
  3. 3 Mash avocados with a fork, adding lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Mash to desired consistency - chunky or smooth.
  4. 4 Spread the mashed avocado generously on each toast slice, making sure to reach the edges.
  5. 5 Top each toast with halved cherry tomatoes and crumbled feta cheese if using.
  6. 6 Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with red pepper flakes for a little kick.
  7. 7 Serve immediately while toast is still warm and crispy. Enjoy!
